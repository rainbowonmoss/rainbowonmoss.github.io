Origin

This recipe has evolved over time in our house and originially it was a lot more complicated with a lot more fresh veggies required.
Now we let the flavours develop using hero ingredients like worstershire sauce, curry powder and bay leaves. These simple cupboard staples go a long way to making this taste like you bought it
We often get excited and make a big batch of sauce for the week, it's the perfect starting ingredient to any red / pink pasta dish.
