Easy Cinnamon Scrolls

Ranking

Takes no time

Origin

The eaisest cinammon scrolls in the world
Keep on hand for a sweet breakfast with coffee or late afternoon snack. Come to think of it, these would make a great desert covered in icecream..mmm



Ingredients

  • Puff pastry sheet - thawed
  • 2 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/3 Cup Brown Sugar
  • Chilli

Method

  • Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius
  • Brush butter to cover entire surface of the puff pastry sheet
  • Mix together cinnamon and brown sugar and sprinkle from edge to edge
  • Press down lightly to help the sugar and cinnamon stick to the butter
  • Roll it up and refrigerate for 20 min
  • Slice the roll in half, in half and in half again to make 8 pieces (see tip 1)
  • Lay on baking paper and bake for 15 minutes or until golden and bubbly

Tips

  • 1. After cutting in half the first time, freeze half to use another day
  • Half roll = 4 scrollls
