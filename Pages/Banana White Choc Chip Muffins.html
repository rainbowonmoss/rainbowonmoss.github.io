Ingredients

  • 2 cups gluten-free self-raising flour
  • ½ cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 75g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 2 medium mashed bananas, (equivalent to 1 cup)
  • ½ cup white choc chip

Method

<

  • Preheat oven to 190C and grease 12-hole muffin tray.
  • Sift the flour into a bowl and stir in the caster sugar.
  • Make a well in the centre.
  • Put the milk, egg and vanilla in a bowl. Whisk and pour into the well.
  • Add the butter, banana and choc chips. Fold until combined, the batter should be lumpy.
  • Divide mixture among the muffin holes and bake for approximately 20 minutes or until lightly golden. Cool in the muffin tray for 5 minutes and then transfer to a wire rack to cool

Tips

  • Try adding some cinammon and maple syrup
  • Make half the batch with white choc and half the batch with milk chocolate chips
