Origin

Perfect as a dip or a side dish for a mexican feast



Ingredients

  • Avos
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Pepper
  • Chilli flakes
  • Coriander
  • Lime juice

Method

  • Crush up the garlic and cover in salt. Rub it around the bowl to get the juices all over it
  • Chuck in everything else and mash until smooth

Tips

  • Add fresh minced garlic if you're reakly keen
